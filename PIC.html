Partners In Crime

Partners in Crime is a multi-media project created by Yazmine Yin, featuring writing, illustration, and animation. Set in the fantasy land of Stravius, the story follows the Clavicymbulums, a notorious band of pirates navigating a life filled with adventure and danger. As they pursue their wanderlust, they must evade authorities, rival criminal factions, and haunting figures from their past.
